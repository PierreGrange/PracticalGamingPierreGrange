-3D environment with different levels
-Goal is to kill a given target (harmless target)
-Guards on the map who will shoot at the player if he gets in their field of view
-Player has to stab the target at close range

Guards :
-Ranged weapons
-Cone field of view
-Area in which they patroll
-The higher the level, the strongest their bullets hit you (at some point you get killed in one shot)

Target :
-Wanders around the area
-Harmless
-Will run away from you if you're in it's field of view
